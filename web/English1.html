    建议你在30分钟内，根据下面所给的题目和提纲用英语写出一篇不少于80词的短文。 Physical Exercise (1) 从事体育运动有哪些好处； (2) 你常做的体育运动。

  • 标准答案：I love sports very much. In my opinion, physical exercise has many advantages. For the first one, it can make us much stronger than before if we do some sports every week. So we can do much better in our work and study. Secondly, doing some sports regularly can make us much healthier. For this, we can live a very long time in our life. For the last one, I think doing sports can let us make a lot of friends with the same interests. In my daily life, I often play basketball, and play football with my best friends. Sometimes I also go swimming with my family. In winter, going skating is also my favorite sport. So I think you should do some sports, too.

    建议你在30分钟内，根据下面所给的题目和提纲用英语写出一篇不少于80词的短文。 Health and Eating Habits (1) 健康和饮食习惯的关系； (2) 你的看法以及理由

  • 标准答案：Eating habit is closely related to health. In order to keep fit, we should pay more attention to our eating habits. My tips on good eating habits are as follows: First, don't eat too much or too little for each meal. Eating too much will cause overweight and eating too little is bad for health too. Second, don't eat between meals. Sometimes we like eating a little before the mealtime; as a result, we lose our appetite for lunch or dinner. Third, eat vegetables and fruits every day. As the saying goes, "An apple a day keeps the doctor away." In my opinion, a good eating habit leads to a healthy life.

    建议你在30分钟内，根据下面所给的题目和提纲用英语写出一篇不少于80词的短文。 How to Learn English Well? (1) 学习方法对英语学习的重要性； (2) 你的英语学习方法。

  • 标准答案：English is always my favorite subject and I am good at English. I would like to share four good studying habits with you. First, remember 20 new words a day, and never give up. Second, go over grammar points I have learnt in English class regularly. Third, read an English article in China Daily every day. Fourth, write an English composition every week and ask teacher to revise that for me to improve the writing level. If you do things above, I am sure your English will also be improved.
